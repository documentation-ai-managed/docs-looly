---

## Overview
Looly projects help organize your documentation.

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Looly dashboard">
    Visit `https://looly.io` and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new project">
    Click "New Project", add a name, then click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start documenting">
    Add pages and sections using the editor. Changes are saved automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
