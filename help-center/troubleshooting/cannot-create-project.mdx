## Issue
- "Create Project" button is disabled or not responding

## Causes
- Not signed in
- Reached project limit
- Browser issues

## Solution
<Steps>
  <Step title="Check sign-in status">
    Sign in at `looly.io` (top right corner).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check project limit">
    Free accounts have maximum 3 projects.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try another browser">
    If issues persist, switch browsers or clear cache.
  </Step>
</Steps>
