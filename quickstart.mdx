## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A team domain if creating a workspace for your organization

</Callout>

Looly works entirely in the browser, so no downloads or installations are required.

## Step 1: Sign Up for Looly

Follow these steps to create your account.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Sign-Up Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [https://app.looly.com/signup](https://app.looly.com/signup).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and a secure password. Alternatively, sign up with Google or GitHub.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Looly and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a few quick questions about your use case to personalize your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">

After signing up, you'll land on your personalized dashboard. Bookmark [https://app.looly.com](https://app.looly.com) for quick access.

</Callout>

## Step 2: Create Your First Space

Spaces organize your documentation projects.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, select **New Space** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Space" icon="edit-3">
    Enter a name like "Project Docs" and choose a visibility: Public, Private, or Team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Add a description, icon, and color (default: #3B82F6). Click **Create**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your new space appears in the sidebar. Click it to enter.

## Step 3: Create Your First Page

Pages are the building blocks of your space.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Markdown Page" icon="file-text">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="New Page" icon="plus">
        Inside your space, click **New Page**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit">
        Type your title and content. Use markdown for formatting.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

````markdown
# Welcome to My Docs

This is your first page!

## Features

- **Rich editing**
- **Version history**
- **Collaboration**
````
